3×3×3 Speed Cube

  • Optimised for speed solving, these cubes allow for better corner cutting and use an adjustable sprung mechanism to make for smoother turns.
  • The cube shown is of the standard 57mm size.
  • Although built for speed-solving, this particular model is not often used in competition in favour of alternative models such as the DaYan ZhanChi or Moyu AoLong V2.
  • The cube was among the first to abandon stickers in favour of more durable tiles, which are also less prone to colour bleaching.
  • According to the World Cube Association, Collin Burns of the USA holds the current single-solve record of 5.25 seconds, while Feliks Zemdegs of Australia holds the 5-solve average record of 6.54 seconds.
